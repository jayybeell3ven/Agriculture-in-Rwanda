Hello this is Johan agriculture page we are here to solve all the main problems of agriculture in Rwanda. There are 3 main things that is causing bad things in Rwanda. these are

  • Lack of capital

These are the main things that are killing Rwanda and lets save this country and make it wayyyy better!

Soil Erosion

This is soil erosion which could cause farmers to lose more money in their job of farming. Soil erosion means a gradual process that occurs when the impact of water or wind detaches and removes soil particles, causing the soil to deteriorate. This could be caused by:

  • hard rain
  • flowing water

Lack of Capital

This is the problem of lack of capital in Rwanda which could cause economic problems. Lack of capital means not having to cover overhead expenses. This could be many reasons for example:

  • lack of saving structures
  • excessive costs
  • devloping capital

Pests and Diseases

This is the problem of pests and diseases which could reproduce virus or infectious disease on the fruits which basically means it is not edible.
The meaning of pests and diseases are insects for example:

  • slug
  • snail
  • nematode
